About the job
3 Month W2 Contract (No C2C/No Visa Sponsorship)

Seeking a Business Analyst with light Project Management experience to support the migration from ServiceNow to a Salesforce-based ITSM platform with an AI-driven interface. This role will partner with stakeholders, gather requirements, manage offshore development team interactions, and support change management efforts related to new features and UI updates. Prior experience with system implementations, migrations, or transformation initiatives is preferred.



We are seeking an experienced Senior Business Analyst with a strong background in business analysis, project management, and OCM/change management. The ideal candidate will have past experience in IT Service Management (ITSM) processes and technologies, with proven capabilities in technology and process implementation or migration. You will serve as a critical liaison between client stakeholders and development teams, ensuring successful delivery of automated ITSM solutions along with organizational change management.


Key Responsibilities:

Coordinate and facilitate communication between client stakeholders and solution development teams, including geographically distributed groups both onshore and offshore.
Analyze client ITSM and CMDB challenges to help refine and optimize proposed automated solutions.
Develop persona-based organizational change management (OCM) artifacts such as communications, training materials, and job aids.
Conduct training sessions for end-users, service desk agents, and resolver groups to ensure smooth adoption of new technologies and processes.


Required Skills and Experience:

Minimum of 8 years overall experience in business analysis.
Proven past experience in project management.
Expertise in change management, organizational change, and stakeholder management.
Knowledge of ITSM processes and technologies.
Hands-on experience with technology and process implementation or migration initiatives.


Preferred Skills:

Experience with ServiceNow platform.
Familiarity with Salesforce.
Knowledge or experience in AI automated solutioning.
