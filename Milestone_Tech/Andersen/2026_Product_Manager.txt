Product Manager
Overview
We are seeking an experienced Product Manager to support the design and build of an Agentic Policy Administration System. The architecture is cloud-native on AWS and leverages agentic patterns to automate and orchestrate policy administration workflows. The product manager will work alongside a cross-functional delivery team including architects, engineers, and client stakeholders to define requirements, manage scope, and drive delivery within a structured engagement model.
The Product Manager will play a critical role in shaping the product vision, driving requirements discovery with business stakeholders, and translating complex insurance lifecycle needs into detailed, developer-ready documentation. This role will also leverage GenAI tools such as Claude to accelerate requirements development, documentation, and collaboration with engineering. An understanding of agentic AI capabilities and where they can appropriately enhance workflow automation will be beneficial.

Key Responsibilities
	Lead and facilitate requirements gathering sessions with business stakeholders and subject matter experts.
	Elicit, clarify, and document business, functional, and technical requirements for a custom policy admin platform.
	Work closely with stakeholders to define and validate the end-to-end policy lifecycle workflow, including key operational challenges and edge cases.
	Translate requirements into detailed markdown documentation for developers, including clear acceptance criteria and technical-level specifications.
	Partner with developers throughout the POC to ensure requirements are understood, prioritized, and delivered effectively.
	Manage developers and coordinate delivery across the project team.
	Use Claude and other GenAI tooling to accelerate requirements writing, documentation, solution shaping, and workflow analysis.
	Collaborate with technical teams to explore how AI and agentic capabilities can be incorporated appropriately into the solution.
	Help define the product direction, MVP scope, and success criteria for the proof-of-life initiative.
	Identify risks, dependencies, and gaps across business and technical workstreams.
	Support alignment across business, technology, and delivery stakeholders in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Required Skills and Experience
	5-10 years’ Product Management experience within a consulting or systems integration context, with demonstrated ability to operate in ambiguous, fast-paced delivery environments.
	Experience supporting or leading delivery of digital or technology-enabled products from requirements definition through functional validation.
	Familiarity with insurance, annuity, or financial services products is required. Understanding of policy lifecycle concepts, benefit mechanics, or related business processes is a plus.
	Strong command of agile delivery practices, including backlog management, requirement development, and sprint facilitation.
	Ability to lead working sessions with technical and business stakeholders to document requirements, surface decisions, and maintain scope discipline.
	Experience translating complex business processes and product rules into structured, executable requirements documentation.
	Demonstrated, habitual use of AI productivity tools, including Claude or Copilot at minimum. This program uses AI tooling as a core component of its delivery workflow. Candidates who cannot speak concretely to day-to-day use of AI tools in their current role are not suitable for this engagement regardless of other qualifications.
