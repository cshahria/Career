Thank you for your possible interest in work2future. 
Work2Future helps Santa Clara County residents gain the skills, guidance, and support they need to find a new job, advance in their current role, or start a new career. We also partner with employers to support workforce development by providing advisory and supportive services. As the local Workforce Development Board, we focus on high-growth industries and in-demand occupations that pay a living wage.
Our services include (but are not limited to):
Job search assistance
Resume preparation and review
Referrals to community resources
Workshops focused on resume development, job search strategies, and interview skills
One-on-one career coaching
Training grants available for up to $7,000 through California’s Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL), which covers tuition and training for adults and dislocated workers
Access to no-cost training in high-demand construction trades through Trade Orientation Programs (TOPs), including Carpentry, HVAC, Plumbing, and more
At Work2Future, our career advisors work closely with each client to provide personalized coaching, training, and work experience to support the next step toward a well-paid job. We offer:
Individualized career coaching and success planning
Resume and interview coaching
Interview and soft skills training through workshops and online learning
Paid work experience opportunities with local employers
Professional certificate training programs

Required Documents
As a government-funded program, our services are provided at no cost. To complete enrollment, we are required to verify your right to work in the U.S. Please bring:
California Driver’s License or a photo ID
Social Security Card (original card only)
If you do not have your Social Security Card, a current U.S. passport or birth certificate
Unemployment Award Letter and last EDD pay stub (if applicable)
DD214 Military Form (if applicable)
Please note: We cannot complete enrollment without these required documents.
