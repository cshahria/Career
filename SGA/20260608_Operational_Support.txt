Software Guidance & Assistance, Inc., (SGA), is searching for an FLEET ADMINISTRATOR/OPS SUPPORT for a CONTRACT assignment with one of our premier UTILITY clients in SAN JOSE, CA.
Onsite 5 days per week.
Responsibilities:
Support daily administrative operations within the Fleet function, including work order processing, documentation, and invoice handling
Review incoming vehicle and equipment deficiency reports, identify duplicates, and escalate safety‐critical issues to leadership as appropriate
Reconcile vendor invoices against maintenance and repair work orders within the asset management system and close completed items
Prepare, code, and submit invoices for payment in accordance with established financial procedures
Enter and reconcile fuel and credit card transactions using fleet payment platforms and internal systems
Upload fuel usage and odometer data from fuel management systems into the enterprise asset management database
Maintain organized records and documentation related to regulatory and compliance requirements, including inspections, emissions testing, and vehicle licensing
Assist with scheduling vehicles and equipment for corrective repairs and preventive maintenance activities
Provide clerical and administrative support to Fleet Supervisors and department leadership as needed
Required Skills:
Experience performing administrative or clerical support functions in a fleet, maintenance, or operations environment
Proficiency with Microsoft Office applications and general comfort working across multiple software systems
Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage high volumes of documentation and transactional data
Attention to detail with the ability to identify discrepancies and ensure accuracy in records and invoices
General understanding of vehicle maintenance, repair processes, or mechanical terminology
Education equivalent to an associate degree, technical training, or relevant work experience in a related field
Preferred Skills:
Experience working with fleet, fuel, or asset management systems
Familiarity with regulatory documentation such as inspection records, emissions compliance, and equipment registration
Prior exposure to fleet maintenance operations involving construction or heavy equipment
Experience coordinating maintenance schedules or supporting preventive maintenance programs
Strong analytical skills with the ability to evaluate issues, consider alternatives, and support operational decision‐making
Background supporting purchasing, inventory coordination, or general services functions
